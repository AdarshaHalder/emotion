## 📋 How to Use:

### **Step 1: Prepare Audio**
Get 1-5 audio recordings:
- Voice memos from phone
- Video clips (extract audio)
- Voice messages
- Phone recordings
- Or record new ones!

**Requirements:**
- 30 seconds - 2 minutes each
- Clear audio
- Natural speech
- No background noise

---

### **Step 2: Clone in App**

1. Open desktop app
2. Sidebar → **"Clone a Voice"**
3. Expand **"Add Family Member Voice"**
4. Enter name: "Mom", "Dad", etc.
5. Upload audio files
6. Click **"Clone Voice"**
7. Wait 1-2 minutes ⏳
8. Done! ✅

---

### **Step 3: Use Cloned Voice**

1. Select cloned voice from dropdown
2. Talk to AI normally
3. **AI responds in your loved one's voice!** 🎊

---
