# 🎙️ VOICE CLONING GUIDE - Clone Family Members' Voices

## 🌟 What is Voice Cloning?

**Clone the voices of your loved ones** (Mom, Dad, Wife, Husband, etc.) so the AI companion can speak in THEIR voice! This creates a more personal and comforting experience.

---

## ✨ How It Works:

```
Audio Samples → ElevenLabs AI → Cloned Voice → AI Speaks
    (You)          (Analyzes)      (Creates)    (In their voice!)
```

---

## 📋 Step-by-Step Guide:

### 1️⃣ **Prepare Audio Samples**

**What you need:**
- 1-5 audio recordings of the person
- Each recording: **30 seconds to 2 minutes**
- Clear audio (no background noise)
- Natural speech (not reading robotically)

**Where to get audio:**
- ✅ Voice memos from phone
- ✅ Video clips (extract audio)
- ✅ Phone call recordings
- ✅ Voice messages
- ✅ Record new samples

**Best practices:**
- Multiple different sentences
- Natural tone and emotion
- Clear pronunciation
- No music/background noise
- Good microphone quality

---

### 2️⃣ **Clone the Voice in App**

1. **Open the desktop app**
2. Go to **Sidebar** → **"Clone a Voice"** section
3. Click **"Add Family Member Voice"**
4. Enter details:
   - **Name**: Mom, Dad, Wife, etc.
   - **Description**: Optional (e.g., "My mother's voice")
5. **Upload audio samples** (1-5 files)
6. Click **"Clone Voice"** button
7. **Wait 1-2 minutes** for processing
8. ✅ **Done!** Voice appears in dropdown

---

### 3️⃣ **Use the Cloned Voice**

1. **Select cloned voice** from dropdown
2. Talk to the AI companion
3. **AI responds in your loved one's voice!** 🎉

## 🔧 Technical Details:

**API Used:** ElevenLabs Professional Voice Cloning  
**Requirements:**
- ElevenLabs account (free tier works!)
- 1-5 audio samples
- MP3 or WAV format

**Processing:**
- Upload time: ~30 seconds
- Clone time: ~1-2 minutes
- Quality: High-fidelity voice match

---

## 💡 Tips for Best Results:

### ✅ DO:
- Use high-quality recordings
- Record in quiet environment
- Natural, conversational speech
- Multiple audio samples (3-5 recommended)
- Different sentences/topics

### ❌ DON'T:
- Use noisy/muffled audio
- Robot-like reading
- Very short clips (<10 seconds)
- Background music/TV
- Distorted audio
